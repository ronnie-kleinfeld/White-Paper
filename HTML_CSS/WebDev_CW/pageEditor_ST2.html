1) Template


The template comprises a navigation bar and footer, both designed to seamlessly adapt to both desktop and mobile views. Hover effects intuitively indicate that the links are clickable. On mobile devices, the navbar remains hidden initially, accessible through a hamburger menu icon. Regardless of device, a clickable logo is present, providing users with a convenient means to return to the home page.


2) Homepage


The homepage features a hero image situated beneath the navigation bar, serving as a visual representation of our focus on affordable energy theme. As visitors scroll down, they encounter informative sections detailing the pages we have developed, each of which is clickable, seamlessly directing users to the relevant content. Notably, within the first section outlining our mission (what we do), users find convenient links to individual team member's content page. In desktop view, these links are accompanied by corresponding images adjacent to the content, while on mobile devices, the images stack neatly atop the content for optimal viewing. This thoughtful design ensures a user-friendly experience, facilitating effortless navigation and enhancing engagement with our platform's offerings.


3) Feedback


On the feedback page, users can share their thoughts and comments about our website. The required fields include "Name", "Email", "Subject" and "Message." If any of these fields are left blank, an error message appears below the respective field, prompting users to provide the necessary information before they can submit the form. Additionally, users can indicate their satisfaction level with the services provided through an optional field. Upon completing the form, users can click the "Next" button to preview their submission. In this second step, users have the option to either edit their form or proceed with submission. If they choose to submit, all the provided information is sent to the designated email address (w1929648@my.westminste.ac.uk). If editing is preferred, the page returns to the form with all previously entered information retained, allowing users to make any necessary adjustments before resubmission.

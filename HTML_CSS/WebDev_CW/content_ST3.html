Sustainable Development Goals

UN Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

Actions

  • Investing in renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydro power
  • Promoting energy efficiency measures in industries, buildings, and transportation
  • Expanding access to electricity in rural and remote areas
  • Supporting research and development of clean energy technologies
Team Page

Targets

  • By 2030, ensure universal access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy services
  • By 2030, increase substantially the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix
  • By 2030, double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency
  • By 2030, enhance international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research and technology
